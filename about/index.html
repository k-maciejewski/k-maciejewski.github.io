About

I earned my Masters in Biostatistics from the Mailman School of Public Health at Columbia University.

I am currently a biostatistician at the Yale Center for Analytics Sciences.

I hope to use data-driven decisions to improve health care and access to care.

I am interested in:

  • Optimized utilization of electronic health records
  • Data visualization
  • Clinical trials design and methods
  • Science education, awareness, accessibility

Tools of the trade:

RStudio, Shiny, SAS, SAS-SUDAAN, SQL, Tableau, MATLAB, and more

Research interests:

  • Big data, data management, data visualization
  • Mental health
  • Maternal health
  • Insurance, access to care, attitudes and barriers to care
